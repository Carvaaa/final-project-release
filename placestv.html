Lisbon

Lisbon, the capital of Portugal, is a hotspot for tourism and for good reason. It shows multiple contrasts between the historical and modern, combining different cultures, trends and lifestyles. It has the typical Portuguese gastronomy and everything portugal has to offer in one city.

Porto

Porto, located at the north of Portugal, was the first capital of the country. Even though it's slightly colder than the other cities, it still has amazing beaches, amazing food and amazing history and architecture along with it, definetely worth visiting.

Algarve

Algarve is located on the south of portugal and is one of Portugal's biggest attractions and for a good reason. It has amazing weather and a hot temperature that makes you visit the stunning beaches it has, with crystal clear water and beatiful rocks. The typical houses there are also beautiful. It's a paradisiac place for a vacation.
